Settings & how this goes live

Right now this is seeded with your existing vault clippings so you can feel the interface.

To go live: the dashboard runs in a sandbox that can't fetch the web itself. Instead, a scheduled Claude task pulls your RSS.app bundle (all your X accounts + Substacks merged into one feed) and refreshes these cards — daily, or whenever you ask. Paste your bundle URL below so it's saved for that task.

Reading state and the “NEW” marker are stored locally in this view and persist across reloads.
