Settings & how this goes live

This page rebuilds automatically. A scheduled GitHub Action pulls your 3 X Lists (via RSSHub) plus your Substacks and blogs, accumulates them into a deduped store, and republishes here — no action needed from you.

Use the lane tabs to switch topics, the type chips to filter X / Substack / Web, and the search box to find anything. The red NEW badge marks items added since your last visit.

Reading state and the “NEW” marker are stored locally on this device and persist across reloads. To add a source, edit feeds.json or add an account to the relevant X List.
